## NOTES FOR CONSISTENCY

**Writing Style:**
- Professional but conversational
- Second person ("you") for engagement
- Short paragraphs for mobile reading
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Bold key points
- Use examples liberally

**Indian Context:**
- Mention tier 2/3 cities where relevant
- Include regional diversity (North/South/East/West preferences)
- Reference popular festivals for timing
- Use local success stories
- Consider language diversity (Hindi, English, regional)

**Avoid:**
- Generic global statistics without India context
- TikTok strategies (banned in India since 2020)
- Dollar pricing
- US-centric examples without adaptation
- Outdated data (pre-2024)
