Common Questions for Creators

Do I need a large following to apply?

+

No! We care about content quality and authenticity, not follower count. Many of our most successful creators started with under 1,000 followers.

How much can I earn?

+

₹2K-₹10K per video depending on complexity and brand requirements. Top creators earn ₹25K-₹75K per month with consistent work.

What equipment do I need?

+

A smartphone with a good camera and basic lighting setup. We provide guidance on affordable equipment upgrades as you grow.

How often will I get projects?

+

Depends on your performance and availability. Active creators typically get 5-10 projects per month. The better your content performs, the more work you'll receive.

Can I work with other agencies?

+

Yes, our partnerships are non-exclusive. We just ask for scheduling transparency so we can match you with the right opportunities.
